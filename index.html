📖 Chief Vet Briefing
Parent Quick Start Guide
Everything you need to run Pet Rescue HQ in under 10 minutes
🩺
🚨 The Mission
A rescue animal is in the clinic and only your child — the Lead Field Medic — can save it. Every homework task completed earns Medicine Tokens. Tokens unlock recovery stages. When the patient reaches Stage 5, they are fully rescued and a certificate is awarded.

You are the Chief Veterinarian — calm, structured, and in charge of the clinic.
⚡ Quick Setup (5 minutes)
Step 1: Print the ID Badge — let your child decorate and wear it during homework time.
Step 2: Print the Patient Chart — fill in together before homework starts.
Step 3: Set out the Medicine Token sheet — visible next to the homework.
Step 4: Flip the Clinic sign to OPEN.
Step 5: Start with ONE subject only — not all of them.
💡 "Do not try to use all the printables on Day 1. Start with the Dashboard and Tokens only. Add tools as your child gets comfortable."
🧠 The Science (Quick Version)
💊 Dopamine Ramp
Small wins release dopamine. Tokens make each win visible. The brain chases the next token — keeping motivation going without nagging.
🧩 Zeigarnik Effect
The greyed-out recovery stages create an "open loop" the brain wants to close. Seeing the unfinished picture drives completion.
🎁 Initial Endowment
Giving 2 free tokens at the start tricks the brain into feeling already begun. Starting is the hardest part — this removes that barrier.
🪞 Mirror Neurons
When YOU stay calm as Chief Vet, your child's brain mirrors that calm. The role-play gives you both a character to hide behind — reducing conflict.
🐾 Remember
"Stop the daily tears over homework. Pet Rescue HQ turns your child's biggest struggle into a rescue mission they actually want to finish."
